# Rent and Payment

1. The rent is {monthly_rent_sgd} per calendar month, payable in advance without demand.
2. Rent is due on day {payment_day} of each calendar month, and shall be paid to the Landlord by bank transfer, PayNow, or such other method as the Landlord may reasonably direct in writing.
3. If any rent remains unpaid seven (7) days after its due date, the Landlord may charge interest on the amount outstanding at a rate of ten percent (10%) per annum, calculated daily from the due date until payment, without prejudice to the Landlord's other rights under this Agreement.
