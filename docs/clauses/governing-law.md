# Governing Law

1. This Agreement is governed by and shall be construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Singapore.
2. The parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Singapore, save that either party may bring a claim in the Small Claims Tribunals where the claim falls within their jurisdiction.
