# Deposit

1. The Tenant shall pay the Landlord a security deposit of {deposit_sgd} (the "Deposit") on or before the commencement of the Term.
2. The Deposit is security for the Tenant's performance of this Agreement. It shall not be treated as payment of rent for any month, including the final month of the Term.
3. Within fourteen (14) days after the Tenant delivers vacant possession of the Premises and returns all keys and access devices, the Landlord shall refund the Deposit without interest, less any sums properly deducted for unpaid rent or outgoings, or for repair of damage beyond fair wear and tear.
4. The Landlord shall provide the Tenant with an itemised list of any deductions.
