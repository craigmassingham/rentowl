# Minor Repair Clause

1. The Tenant shall keep the interior of the Premises, including the fixtures, fittings and household effects, in good and tenantable repair and condition, fair wear and tear excepted.
2. The Tenant shall bear the cost of minor repairs and routine replacements (including light bulbs, tap washers, and servicing of air-conditioning units at intervals of not more than three (3) months) up to {minor_repair_threshold_sgd} per item or occurrence.
3. Where the cost of any single repair exceeds {minor_repair_threshold_sgd}, the Tenant shall bear the first {minor_repair_threshold_sgd} and the Landlord shall bear the excess, provided the damage or defect did not arise from the negligence or default of the Tenant, in which case the Tenant bears the full cost.
4. The Landlord shall be responsible for repairs of a structural nature, and for defects in the electrical wiring and plumbing systems not caused by the Tenant's misuse.
