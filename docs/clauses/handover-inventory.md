# Handover and Inventory

1. On commencement of the Term, the Landlord shall deliver the Premises to the Tenant in clean and tenantable condition, together with an inventory of fixtures, fittings and household effects (the "Inventory") signed by both parties.
2. The Tenant shall notify the Landlord in writing of any discrepancy in the Inventory or any defect in the Premises within fourteen (14) days of commencement, failing which the Premises and Inventory are deemed accepted in good condition.
3. At the end of the Term, the Tenant shall return the Premises and all items in the Inventory in the same condition as at handover, fair wear and tear excepted, and shall return all keys and access devices.
