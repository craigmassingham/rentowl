# Utilities and Charges

1. The Tenant shall pay all charges for electricity, water, gas, internet, telephone, and television services supplied to the Premises during the Term, and shall register the relevant utility accounts in the Tenant's own name where applicable.
2. The Landlord shall pay all property tax, and all maintenance, conservancy or management charges payable in respect of the Premises (including management corporation contributions, where applicable).
3. Each party shall promptly provide the other with evidence of payment of the charges for which it is responsible, on reasonable request.
