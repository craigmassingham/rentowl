# Use of Premises

1. The Tenant shall use the Premises as a private residence for the occupiers named or permitted under this Agreement only, and not for any business, trade, or illegal or immoral purpose.
2. The Tenant shall not assign, sublet, or part with possession of the Premises or any part of it without the Landlord's prior written consent.
3. The Tenant shall comply with all laws, and with all rules, by-laws and regulations applicable to the Premises, including (where applicable) the rules of the Housing & Development Board and the by-laws of the management corporation.
4. The Tenant shall ensure that every occupier of the Premises is lawfully resident in Singapore, and shall provide the Landlord with copies of relevant immigration passes on request.
5. The Tenant shall not keep any pet at the Premises without the Landlord's prior written consent, and in the case of HDB premises, only pets permitted by the Housing & Development Board may be kept.
