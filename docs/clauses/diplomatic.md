# Diplomatic Clause

1. If, after {diplomatic_min_stay_months} months of the Term, the Tenant (or the named occupier whose employment supports the tenancy) is transferred out of Singapore permanently, is refused permission to remain in Singapore, or ceases to be employed in Singapore, the Tenant may terminate this Agreement by giving the Landlord not less than {diplomatic_notice_months} months' written notice, or rent in lieu of notice.
2. The Tenant shall provide documentary evidence of the transfer, refusal, or cessation of employment from the relevant employer or authority together with the notice.
3. On termination under this clause, the Landlord may recover from the Deposit a pro-rated portion of any agent's commission paid by the Landlord, calculated over the unexpired portion of the Term.
