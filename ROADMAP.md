# ROADMAP.md — what's in scope, what's out

> Derived from the sprint plans in `/sprint-plans/`. Updated monthly after each retro.

## The 4-month MVP

| Month | Theme | Headline outcome |
|---|---|---|
| M1 | Foundation + contract engine | Landlord signs up, adds property + tenancy, generates an IEA-style TA PDF. 5 pilots onboarded. |
| M2 | Payments tracking + reminders | Rent cycles tracked, PayNow QR per cycle, WhatsApp reminders firing. 10 pilots, ≥1 real payment reconciled. |
| M3 | AI maintenance triage | Tenant PWA reports issues; AI classifies severity, cause, responsibility per TA. ≥80% landlord agreement rate. |
| M4 | Polish, pricing, public launch | Stripe subscriptions live, 10+ paying users, MRR ≥ S$150, public landing + blog. |
